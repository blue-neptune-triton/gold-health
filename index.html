Understanding Regenerative Therapy

Regenerative therapies harness cells that are being studied for their potential to support the body’s natural repair processes. Researchers are exploring how mesenchymal stem cells may help modulate inflammation and encourage tissue restoration under clinical supervision.

At Gold Health, these therapies are offered within observational studies or approved research protocols. Individual experiences vary, and there are no guaranteed outcomes.

Why Mexico for Regenerative Therapy?

Regulatory Framework: Mexico requires stem cell procedures to take place within sanctioned research protocols that are reviewed by independent ethics committees and sanctioned by COFEPRIS. The 2016 biotechnology standards enforce rigorous quality control measures including cell viability, sterility and potency testing ([SCA LRA Update - April 2025: Regulatory Landscape of ...](https://www.isctglobal.org/telegrafthub/blogs/ken-ip1/2025/04/09/sca-lra-update-april-2025-regulatory-landscape-of#:~:text=SCA%20LRA%20Update%20,and%20the%20National%20Health%20Council), [Weighing the Pros and Cons: Exploring Medical Care in ...](https://www.magazine.medicaltourism.com/article/weighing-the-pros-and-cons-exploring-medical-care-in-mexico#:~:text=Is%20Stem%20Cell%20Therapy%20in,highest%20level%20of%20therapeutic%20effectiveness)).

Cost & Access: Medical care in Mexico can be significantly more affordable than in the U.S. while maintaining high standards ([The Benefits of Medical Tourism in Mexico](https://clichemag.com/medical/the-benefits-of-medical-tourism-in-mexico-a-comprehensive-overview/#:~:text=The%20Benefits%20of%20Medical%20Tourism,Conclusion)). Short travel times from North America and bilingual staff make visits convenient.

Experience: Our clinic employs physicians trained in the U.S., Canada or Europe and offers modern facilities, adhering to ethical sourcing and traceability requirements.

Cultural Experience: Enjoy rest and recovery in a warm, welcoming environment along Mexico’s beautiful coast.

Safety, Ethics & Compliance

We operate within Mexico’s legal and ethical framework. Article 327 prohibits commercialization of organs, tissues or cells, and Article 330 permits stem cell transplants only after scientific evaluation of risks and benefits ([13 tips for establishing and building trust around health ...](https://hsph.harvard.edu/research/health-communication/resources/tips-for-establishing-trust/#:~:text=Stem%20cell%20therapy%20laws%20in,a%20tissue%20for%20medical%20use)). Our therapies are offered only under approved research protocols and observational studies.

The 2016 biotechnology standards require testing for cell viability, sterility and potency ([Weighing the Pros and Cons: Exploring Medical Care in ...](https://www.magazine.medicaltourism.com/article/weighing-the-pros-and-cons-exploring-medical-care-in-mexico#:~:text=Is%20Stem%20Cell%20Therapy%20in,highest%20level%20of%20therapeutic%20effectiveness)). We use ethically sourced autologous or allogeneic cells with full traceability and quality assurance. All procedures are performed by licensed physicians, and we encourage patients to consult their primary healthcare providers before participating.
