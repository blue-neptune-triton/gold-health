Understanding Regenerative Therapy

Regenerative therapy is an umbrella term for treatments that aim to stimulate the body’s natural healing processes. Researchers are exploring how cellular therapies and biologics may support tissue repair and inflammation modulation. At Gold Health, we guide you through what these therapies involve and ensure you have realistic expectations.

Why Mexico?

Mexico offers progressive regulations that allow access to regenerative therapies still under study in other countries. Gold Health operates under COFEPRIS authorization to provide compliant, ethical treatments. Our geographic location also makes it an accessible and cost-effective destination for patients from the U.S. and beyond.

Safety & Ethics

Your safety is our top priority. We adhere to all regulations set by Mexican health authorities and ensure that our therapies use ethically sourced materials. Our staff will provide you with thorough information about potential risks, and we never claim guaranteed results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is regenerative therapy?

Regenerative therapy refers to a range of treatments that use cells or biologics to support the body’s repair mechanisms. While promising, these therapies are still being studied to understand their safety and efficacy.

Is regenerative therapy safe?

Gold Health follows strict protocols and operates under COFEPRIS approval. We only source materials from certified labs and maintain rigorous quality control. However, as with any medical intervention, there are potential risks which we will discuss transparently during your consultation.

What can I expect after treatment?

Every patient responds differently. Some may experience improvement in their condition, while others may see minimal change. Our team will stay in touch to monitor your progress and provide ongoing support.
